About

Santa Lucia Press is a purveyor of extra fine printed materials and design. We specialize in the creation of letterpress and screenprinted products that combine the beauty of traditional type with unique qualities of hand drawn imagery. Our in-house services include: job printing, custom design, and illustration. If you have questions about a project or would like to receive a price quote, please email us at sanluciapress@gmail.com. Santa Lucia Press is owned and operated by K.B. Sawyer out of Portland, Oregon.
